About the

Town of Pegram Park

Come join us, at both work & play

Pegram has one-multi-purpose Park. Many of the Town's larger events are held in the park area, including the annual Pegram Independence Day Celebration, annual Easter Egg Hunt and many other year round special events..

Pegram Park includes walking tracks, three ball fields, bating cage, a covered picnic area with shelter and fireplace,
